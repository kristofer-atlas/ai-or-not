🔍 How to Spot AI-Generated Images

👀 Strange Eyes

AI often struggles with eyes - look for odd reflections, mismatched colors, or unnatural pupil shapes.

✋ Weird Hands

Hands have too many or too few fingers, unusual positions, or blurry fingers fused together.

🔲 Odd Textures

Look for smudged text, distorted logos, or impossible textures in backgrounds.

💇 Hair Problems

AI hair often looks too perfect, has strand inconsistencies, or wrong directions of flow.

🌈 Unnatural Colors

Watch for impossible color combinations, odd skin tones, or overly saturated hues.

🔗 Asymmetries

Check for mismatched earrings, uneven eyebrows, or asymmetric facial features.
